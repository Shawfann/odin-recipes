Chai Pomegranate Overnight Oats

Overnight oats with chai tea latte, vanilla yogurt, honey, and pomegranate seeds make a perfect on-the-go breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1 chai tea bag
  • 1/4 cup of vanilla Greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • 1/4 cup of pommegranate seeds

Method

  1. Make Chai Tea Latte by adding Chai tea to milk. Allow to cool.
  2. Add oats to container of your choice and pour in Chai Tea Latte.
  3. Layer on yogurt and top with honey and pomegranate seeds.
  4. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
